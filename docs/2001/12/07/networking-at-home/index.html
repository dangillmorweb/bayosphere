Networking at Home

Posted on December 7, 2001 by Dan Gillmor

I’ve gotten my router set up at home, and it’s working well. Today I had three computers — Windows 2000, Mac (OS 9.2. and X) and Linux (Mandrake) — running like a charm off my DSL connection. DHCP is a wonderful thing.

People who know my usual luck with computers are shaking their heads with amazement at this point. If there’s a bug I can always find it.

Now back to your regularly scheduled program…
